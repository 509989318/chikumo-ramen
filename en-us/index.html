Our values

Contrary to your conventional restaurant, a ramen shop can tell a whole story through a single bowl of ramen.

An inclusive space for all, where everyone can feel welcome and enjoy a satisfying meal at an affordable price.

Our wish is to share the amazing Japanese food culture that is ramen with the rest of the world.

To touch the hearts and stomachs of everyone in the world through a single bowl of ramen.

With this value in mind, our team at Menya Chikumo continues to prepare ramen day after day.

Our commitment to quality chicken

No chemical seasonings or additives are used in our Chicken Paitan ramen.

Our dedication to sticking to all-natural ingredients is what fully brings out the chicken "umami" in the broth that we serve.

After a whole chicken is slow-cooked overnight, each bone is completely chopped up until none is left behind.

Please enjoy our signature hearty Chicken Paitan soup, full of umami.
